In 2o; upright format. Hand-ruled paper, 12 staves in light brown ink and music in dark brown ink. A clean copy without corrections.

Works D.1.2 and D.1.3 are archived under a single shelfmark. The title page only refers to D.1.3, since it describes a motet for four voices. Yet, D.1.3 was likely added at a later point in time, as evident from the org part: Its first page, as well as staves 1–4 of the second page contain music for D.1.2, while music for D.1.3 is written on staves 5–12 in a darker ink.
